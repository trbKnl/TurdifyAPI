Anonymize your pictures with turdify-api

Do you want to share your photo's on the gram, but you are afraid people will identify you?! Fear no more. I present to you turdify-api you can anonymize your photos, using state of the A.R.T machine learning and A.I. Now you can participate on the world wide web without having to be afraid.

Try it out for yourself!

Usage with curl

This line shows you how you would POST your image to the API using curl and to save the result.

curl -v -F 'my_file=@/path/to/image/with/face.png' https://turdify.niekdeschipper.com/turdify/ > facewithturd.png
